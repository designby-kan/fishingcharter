Captain Carlos Alberto Arevalo

My name is Captain Carlos Arevalo and fishing has been my passion ever since i was a little boy. Living here on the West Coast of Florida has provided me and my guides some of the best backwater fishing in Florida. Hickspanic Outdoor Fishing Charters operated by myself Captain Carlos we target a wide varitey on inshore spieces as well as offer scalloping (During season) and Sunset tours.

My number one goal is to provide you with a memoriable fishing experience that wil last a lifetime. Our targeted spieces can be found up and down the coast from citrus County down to St. Pete to include all of the Tampa Bay (both Pinalles and Tampa side).If your looking to chase those rolling Tarpons during the summer, We can target those fast action and jumping Tarpons. If your just looking to get some fish in the cooler for dinner we can target a variety of spieces with live bait and artifcial.

Targeted Species

Weather yours looking to spend either Half day or whole day out targeting redfish, snook, trout and more.

Scalloping

The gulf coast offer some of the best scalloping during the summer, great way to spend the day with the family out on the water snorkling and limiting out of some delicious scallops.

Sunset Tours

The west coast of Florida gives us some of the most beautiful sunsets great for winding down from the week or capturing that picture perfect photo.
